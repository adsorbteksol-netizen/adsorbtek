What Are Pharmaceutical Desiccants?

Pharmaceutical desiccants are moisture-absorbing materials used in the interior of pharmaceutical bottles, blister packs, sachets, and other pharmaceutical packaging. They serve the main purpose of maintaining dryness so that moisture does not have an impact on medicine.

A wide range of pharmaceutical products are hygroscopic, which is defined as a tendency to absorb moisture from the air. If not properly protected, humidity can cause:

  • Reduced drug potency

  • Chemical degradation

  • Softening or cracking of the tablet

  • Capsule swelling

  • Shortened shelf life

  • Increased contamination risk

By ensuring the medicine's stability and quality in the manufacturing process as well as during its entire lifecycle, pharmaceutical desiccants enable manufacturers to build in drug stability and packaging performance.

Why Is It That Moisture Control Is Important in Pharma Packaging?

Humidity is among the primary factors that cause degradation of pharmaceutical products. Changes in moisture, which may be slight or moderate, can result in stability changes of the active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and even affect the drug's function.

With effective moisture control in pharma packaging, manufacturers can:

  • Preserve drug efficacy

  • Extend shelf life

  • Reduce moisture-related damage

  • Maintain product appearance

  • Conduct safe storage and transport

Appropriate moisture control in pharma packaging can help pharmaceutical companies to reduce product loss and enhance patient safety.

Types of Pharmaceutical Desiccants

Each medicine has its own moisture protection solution based on its sensitivity and packaging type.

Silica Gel for Medicine

Silica gel for medicine is the most popular medicine desiccant due to its high water absorbability and chemical stability. It does not have any toxicities, smell, or is harmful for solid dosage forms like tablets and capsules. It is used in pharmaceutical packaging, which is a preferred choice because of its prolonged performance.

Molecular Sieves

Molecular sieve is very efficient for moisture removal from highly sensitive medicines, biologics, and diagnostic products, which require very low humidity levels.

Clay Desiccants

Clay desiccants are a reliable choice that provide moisture protection for regular pharmaceutical and nutraceutical packaging and are an economical medium.

Calcium Oxide Desiccants

These desiccants are usually used in special pharmaceutical products where better humidity protection is needed.
